National Students' Space Challenge

National Students' Space Challenge is India's one and only space science and engineering competition. It is held every year at IIT Kharagpur by Space Technology Students' Society (spAts) in association with Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO). Are you a space enthusiast? Looking for a first-hand experience at solving some of the real-time challenges faced by engineers all over the world? Just want to have a good time learning stuff about space technology and catching up on astronomy? Then, NSSC is just the thing for you! *Competitions:
